UPLOAD ARTWORK

The Academic Promotions Team has made it easy for Sponsors to upload artwork. Please take a moment to send us any logos, ad copy, fonts, graphics or specifics for your ad space. For artwork specifications please refer to the information below the form.

Below is a simple form that will allow you to fill out the required information along with the artwork you want displayed on the ad space that you purchased. It's easy and hassle free. Please fill out thoroughly.

In case of any difficulty please contact our customer support for further help.

ATTENTION

Do not navigate away from this page until you click Confirm on the Confirmation page, otherwise your upload may not be processed. Depending on the size of your files, if you upload artwork, it may take a few minutes to process.

Upload file size limit is 750 KB

If your file exceeds 750 KB, please send your artwork to info@leratechit.com

We strive to provide our customers with the best customer service by delivering you products as quickly as possible. If you do not receive a proof within 5 business days, just give us a ring at 855 720 2625 or email us at info@leratechit.com

Thank you for your patience.

ARTWORK SPECIFICATIONS

Please send your artwork according to the specifications below:

FORMAT:
  • EPS*
  • PDF*
  • JPEG
RESOLUTION:

If your artwork is a jpeg or bitmap set your image resolution to 300 dpi at finished size.

PRE PRESS:

If your artwork is vector(EPS, PDF, etc.) please convert all fonts to objects(curves, outlines) and embed any linked images.

FONTS:

Please include the name(s) or file(s) of standard fonts that your company uses.

COLORS:

All spirit items will be printed in the high school colors that you sponsored. (With the exception of banners which are printed in full color).
