WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO

Academic Promotions is a marketing firm that works closely with the local business community and high schools, universities, youth organizations across the country. Our focus is on helping schools create a better student experience through involvement from the local business community. Those businesses that are featured on the specialty items make it possible to provide free products like T-Shirts, tote bags, or rally towels to each school or organization at ABSOLUTELY NO-COST.

We work directly with the businesses in your area that want to reach your demographic as well as a desire to bring awareness to their local brands. We provide quality promotional products that the student body and your community will actually use. In doing so, schools are able to distribute great spirit items and other great products as giveaways or fundraisers while the businesses are able to advertise and sponsor at the same time.

Once the school or organization has signed up and selected the items they need, our Project Coordinators will contact local businesses to gain support. We then gather the artwork and create amazing graphics. After all the items have been printed, we will then send them over to the schools and the samples to the sponsors. The cost of the material, production, printing and shipping is provided free of charge to the school thanks to the advertising businesses.
